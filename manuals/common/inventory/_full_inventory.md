## Process: Full Inventory

**Full Inventory** involves a detailed count and verification of all stock in the warehouse to ensure that physical stock matches system records. It is conducted periodically to ensure accuracy, identify discrepancies, optimize stock levels, and aid in compliance and decision-making. [cite: 534, 536, 537, 538, 539, 540]

### How to View and Download Full Inventory

1. **Navigate**: Go to **Inventory > Full Inventory**. 
2. **View Inventory**: The system displays the total inventory, often categorized by stock type like **Good** or **Bad** stock. 
3. **Download Data**:
  * Click **"Click here to download full inventory"** to get the complete data in a standard file format. 
  * Click the **Export** button to download details with advanced filters for further analysis.
